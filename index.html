01 — ABOUT

Applied AI, built to ship

AI/ML Engineer building production-ready AI systems with Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), AI agents, and Computer Vision. I'm passionate about turning cutting-edge AI research into reliable, scalable applications that solve real-world problems.

From designing retrieval pipelines and integrating LLMs to training deep learning models and deploying full-stack AI applications, I enjoy owning the complete development lifecycle. My technical focus includes LangChain, LangGraph, semantic search, vector databases, multimodal AI, FastAPI, and modern ML deployment.

My recent work includes a bilingual legal AI assistant that makes Pakistani law more accessible through RAG and LLMs, and an AI-powered snake identification system using deep learning and explainable AI. Both projects were built end-to-end—from data preparation and model development to backend APIs, user interfaces, and deployment.

I was selected for the Samsung Innovation Campus AI Training Program at COMSATS University Islamabad, where I completed an intensive AI curriculum. I'm currently expanding my expertise in agentic AI systems, production LLM applications, and scalable machine learning infrastructure while actively seeking opportunities to build impactful AI products.
